Dr. Sanduk Ruit

Sanduk Ruit is an ophthalmologist (eye surgeon) from Nepal who has restored the sight of over 130,000 people across Africa and Asia using small-incision cataract surgery.He is also one of the founders of the Tilganga Institute of Ophthalmology.
For his work in taking quality, life-transforming cataract surgery to the poorest, he has been referred to as the "God of Sight".
